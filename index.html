Telco Customer Churn Analysis

1 in 4 customers was leaving. The business needed to know who was at risk and why, before they cancelled, not after.

  • Ended with segment-level retention plays, not just a model score, so the output was something a marketing team could actually use

Python · Machine Learning · Group Project

Airbnb Price Prediction, Brisbane

Predicting what a Brisbane Airbnb should cost, across 3,700+ listings with wildly different property types, locations, and host behaviours. Part of a university Kaggle competition, I was responsible for all the modelling.

  • XGBoost outperformed Linear Regression and Random Forest, not surprising given how non-linear Airbnb pricing is across neighbourhoods and property types
  • Airbnb prices are heavily skewed, a few luxury listings drag the average up significantly. Transforming the target variable before modelling was the fix that made predictions stable
  • Used cross-validation throughout so model comparisons were honest, not just fit to the training data
  • Landed at #96 out of 140 teams on the Kaggle leaderboard, graded on methodology, not rank. Completed in collaboration with Sakshi and Bindu

SQL · Database Design

Urban Eats Café: Built from the Ground Up

Most projects start with a dataset. This one started with nothing. The entire database for a fictional multi-outlet café chain was designed, built, and analysed from scratch, turning a blank schema into real operational insights.

  • Designed 11 tables with proper relationships, primary and foreign keys, and cardinality. Started from a blank page, not an existing schema
  • Urban Eats Hub led in total sales at $514 while Urban Eats Alley trailed at $383. The gap pointed to a footfall and location problem, not a product one
  • Beef Burger was the runaway favourite across all outlets. Iced Latte and Chicken Wings barely moved. A clear signal to rationalise the menu and double down on what customers actually order
  • Three employees had no employment status on record. A small data gap with real compliance and payroll consequences if left unaddressed
